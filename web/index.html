UIUCTF

UIUCTF is an upcoming CTF this spring, brought to you by the students of SIGPony at the University of Illinois in Urbana Champaign (UIUC). UIUCTF 2015 is scheduled to take place 7PM Friday, the 3rd of April for 24 hours until 7PM Saturday the 4th of April (Central Standard Time). Right now we are still in the development stages of the event, so check our frequently asked questions or twitter for more details.
